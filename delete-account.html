TennisCoach AI – Account Deletion Request

If you would like to delete your TennisCoach AI account and all associated data, you can request deletion at any time using the instructions below.

How to Request Account Deletion

To delete your account, please email our support team at:

tenniscoachai.team@gmail.com

Include the subject line: “Account Deletion Request” and provide the email address associated with your TennisCoach AI account.

What Data Will Be Deleted

Once your request is processed, the following data will be permanently deleted:

  • Your account and login credentials
  • Your saved drills, strategies and playbook items
  • Your practice logs and progress tracking data
  • Your preferences and app settings

What Data May Be Retained

Some data may be retained for legal, security or financial compliance reasons, including:

  • Purchase history (required by Google Play)
  • Subscription status and billing records

How Long Deletion Takes

Account deletion requests are processed within 7 days. You will receive a confirmation email once your data has been deleted.
